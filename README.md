# Polar Customers Map 🗺️🌍

![Cover](public/cover.png)

Visualize your customers around the world!

Fetches orders directly from your Polar organization and highlights every country where you've ever had paying customers.

- Ranks countries by revenue
- Exports to PNG
- Excludes trials and $0 orders
- 100% local: all requests to Polar are made from your browser, and your auth token is never stored

Currently, only [Polar](https://polar.sh) is supported, but additional platforms may be added in the future.
